--- type: "manual" title: "IOINIT" linkTitle: "IOINIT" description: "Initialise I/O Devices" subtopic: "sys" categories: - c64 - kernalAPI api: - name: "IOINIT" addr: "FF84" title: "Initialise I/O Devices" ---

This routine initializes all input/output devices and routines.

It is normally called as part of the initialization procedure of a Commodore 64 program cartridge.
