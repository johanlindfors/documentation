Resizing a video

A common task is to resize a video captured on a Mobile Phone to a smaller format that's acceptable to social media like Mastodon. Most services have a file size limit like 50Mb so if your mobile has faithfully captured video at 4K then that won't upload.

So a quick option is to resize the video's physical size. The following will resize it to 480p which works most of the time.

{{< highlight go "linenos=inline" >}} ffmpeg -i source.mp4 -s 720x480 smaller.mp4 {{< /highlight >}}
