--- type: "manual" title: "SRA Rotate bits right with Carry" linkTitle: "SRA" description: "Rotate bits right with carry, bit 7 remains unchanged" tags: - z80 instruction --- {{< z80/instruction def="/l SRA r/11001011 CB/00101r//l SRA (HL)/11001011 CB/00101110 2E//l SRA (IX+d)/11011101 DD/11001011 CB/d/00101110 2E//l SRA (IY+d)/11111101 FD/11001011 CB/d/00101110 2E" >}}

An arithmetic shift right 1 bit position is performed on the contents of operand. The contents of bit 0 are copied to the Carry flag and the previous contents of bit 7 remain unchanged.
