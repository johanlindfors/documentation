--- type: "manual" title: "Overview" linkTitle: "Overview" weight: 1 description: > History of the 6502 family of microprocessors ---

The 6502 is the most common of the 65xx family of 8-bit & 16-bit microprocessors based on the Motorola 6800.

Introduced in 1975 the entire family became popular with the early home computers including the Commodore PET, VIC-20, C64, Apple II, Atari 800 & the BBC Microcomputer system.
