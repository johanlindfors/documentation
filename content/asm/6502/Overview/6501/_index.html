--- type: "manual" title: "6501" linkTitle: "6501" weight: 10 description: MOS Technology 6501 ---

The MOS Technology 6501 was a variant of the 6502 and released at the same time in 1975.

It was pin compatible with the Motorola 6800 whilst the more popular 6502 had a different layout to support an on-chip oscillator.

Both processors could work with the 6800 processor's support chips.
